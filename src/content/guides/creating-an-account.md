Creating your account is quick and easy. You can sign up from any of our platforms and your data will sync seamlessly across all devices.

## Available Platforms

Budget Warden is available on multiple platforms:

- **Web App** - Access from any browser at <a href="https://app.budgetwarden.com" target="_blank">app.budgetwarden.com</a>
- **iOS App** - Download from the <a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/budget-warden/id6741514133" target="_blank">App Store</a>
- **Android App** - Download from the <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lazarovco.budgetwarden&pcampaignid=web_share" target="_blank">Google Play Store</a>

You can create an account from any of these platforms, and your account will work across all of them.

## Sign-Up Methods

Budget Warden offers multiple ways to create your account:

### Email Sign-Up

Create an account using an email address specifically for Budget Warden:

1. Open Budget Warden on your preferred platform
2. Click or tap **"Sign Up with Email"**
3. Enter your details and complete the form
4. Start budgeting

### Google Account

Sign up quickly using your existing Google account:

1. Open Budget Warden on your preferred platform
2. Click or tap **"Continue with Google"**
3. Select your Google account
4. Grant Budget Warden permission to access your basic profile information
5. You might be prompted to input some additional details if they are missing in your Google account
6. Start budgeting

### Apple Account

Sign up using your Apple ID for enhanced privacy:

1. Open Budget Warden on your preferred platform
2. Click or tap **"Continue with Apple"**
3. Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
4. Choose whether to share or hide your email address
5. You might be prompted to input some additional details if they are missing in your Google account
6. Start budgeting

This option is available on the web and iOS apps, not available on Android.

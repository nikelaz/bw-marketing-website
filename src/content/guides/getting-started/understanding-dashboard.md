# Understanding Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is the command center for your financial life. Here's a breakdown of what you'll find.

## Overview Section

At the top of your dashboard, you'll see:

- **Current Month Summary** - Income vs. expenses at a glance
- **Budget Health Score** - A quick indicator of how well you're sticking to your budget
- **Upcoming Bills** - Bills due in the next 7 days

## Spending Breakdown

The spending breakdown shows where your money is going:

- Visual pie chart of spending by category
- Comparison to budgeted amounts
- Trend indicators (up or down from last month)

## Recent Transactions

A live feed of your most recent transactions, automatically categorized and ready for review.

## Quick Actions

Common tasks are just one click away:

- Add transaction
- Transfer between accounts
- Adjust budget
- Generate report

## Customizing Your Dashboard

You can customize your dashboard by:

1. Clicking the **gear icon** in the top right
2. Dragging widgets to rearrange them
3. Toggling widgets on or off
4. Saving your layout

# Managing Your Subscription

Learn how to manage your Budget Warden subscription settings.

## Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. View your current plan, billing cycle, and next billing date

## Available Plans

### Free Plan
- 1 budget
- 2 linked accounts
- Basic reports
- Email support

### Premium Plan
- Unlimited budgets
- Unlimited linked accounts
- Advanced reports & analytics
- Priority support
- SMS notifications
- Data export

### Family Plan
- Everything in Premium
- Up to 5 family members
- Shared budgets
- Family dashboard

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **"Upgrade"**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm the upgrade

Your new features will be available immediately.

## Canceling Your Subscription

We're sorry to see you go! To cancel:

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Tell us why you're leaving (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

**Note:** You'll retain access to premium features until the end of your billing period. Your data is kept for 30 days after cancellation.

## Billing Questions

For billing inquiries, contact our support team with your account email and we'll assist you promptly.

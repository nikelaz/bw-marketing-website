# Setting Up Notifications

Stay on top of your finances with Budget Warden's notification system.

## Available Notification Types

### Bill Reminders
Get notified before bills are due so you never miss a payment.

- 7 days before due date
- 3 days before due date
- Day of due date
- Overdue alerts

### Budget Alerts
Know when you're approaching or exceeding your budget limits.

- 50% of budget used
- 75% of budget used
- 90% of budget used
- Budget exceeded

### Transaction Notifications
Stay informed about account activity.

- Large transactions (customizable threshold)
- Unusual spending patterns
- New recurring charges detected

## Notification Channels

Choose how you want to receive notifications:

- **Email** - Detailed notifications with context
- **Push** - Real-time alerts on your mobile device
- **In-App** - Notifications within Budget Warden
- **SMS** - Text messages for critical alerts (Premium)

## Configuring Notifications

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Toggle notification types on or off
3. Set your preferred channels for each type
4. Customize thresholds where applicable
5. Save your preferences

# Shared Budgets

Budget Warden makes it easy to manage finances together with family members or partners.

## Why Share a Budget?

- **Transparency** - Everyone sees the same financial picture
- **Accountability** - Track spending as a team
- **Goals** - Work together toward shared financial goals
- **Simplicity** - One budget instead of trying to coordinate multiple

## Setting Up a Shared Budget

### Step 1: Create or Select a Budget
You can share an existing budget or create a new one specifically for sharing.

### Step 2: Invite Members
1. Open your budget settings
2. Click **"Share Budget"**
3. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite
4. Select their permission level
5. Send the invitation

### Step 3: Accept Invitation
The invited person will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation and join the budget.

## Permission Levels

| Permission | View | Add Transactions | Edit Budget | Manage Members |
|------------|------|------------------|-------------|----------------|
| Viewer     | Yes  | No               | No          | No             |
| Contributor| Yes  | Yes              | No          | No             |
| Editor     | Yes  | Yes              | Yes         | No             |
| Admin      | Yes  | Yes              | Yes         | Yes            |

## Best Practices

- Start with a discussion about financial goals
- Decide on spending limits that work for everyone
- Schedule regular budget review meetings
- Use categories that make sense for your household
